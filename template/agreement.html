THE PARTIES HEREBY AGREE AS FOLLOWS:

  1. The subject property is legally owned by the company.
  2. The company shall reserve in the name of the OEpartner a part of the said property commensurate to the amount paid by the OEpartner.
  3. The OEpartner agrees to meet up with the goal set as contained in "b" above.
  4. Property prices are subject to upward review at intervals due to value appreciation, the OEpartner hereby confirms awareness to this information, acquires as many units upon first purchase and agrees to acquire more units at their going rate.
  5. The company shall open two wallets in the name of the OEpartner; a Cash Wallet and a Property Wallet.
  6. The initial payment made and all other subsequent payments to be made by the OEpartner in order to acquire more units shall be credited into the OEpartner's property wallet.
  7. The cash wallet will have a zero opening balance and money can be added to it either by earning Referral-Reward or by depositing self cash into it.
  8. The OEpartner shall have full access to the cash wallet and will be able to either withdraw it for personal use or transfer it to the Property Wallet at will.
  9. The amount in the Property Wallet reflects the property stake of the OEpartner.
  10. The property wallet fund will be used to increase the OEpartner's property ownership stake, acquire new units, or develop his/her property (in case of land)... within the company's owned properties and can neither be withdrawn by the OEpartner nor used for any other purpose.
  11. Upon the execution of this agreement, the company shall add the OEpartner to an online platform where property value appreciation, tips, property investment education etc. will be updated
  12. The OEpartner shall enjoy a 10% discount within the duration of this partnership on all one-time-outright/first-payment-instalment purchases made on the company products: Estate Land & Building, Property Verification, Professional Advisory and Design Build & Manage Services.
  13. The OEpartner shall further enjoy a 10% commission on the one-time-outright/first-payment-instalment purchases made by all buyers referred by the OEpartner within the duration of this partnership.
  14. Each commission shall be split into 5 equal parts; 1/5 to be credited into the Cash Wallet and 4/5 to be credited into the Property Wallet.
  15. The Units' OWNERSHIP can only be transferred (sell, gift, collateral etc) after a minimum period of 24 month from the date of the execution of this agreement.
  16. The OEpartnership has an expiration period of 24 months effective from the date of the execution of this agreement, thereafter, the OEpartner will be required to renew the partnership by purchasing new own-earner units in addition to his/her existing ownership stake.
  17. If the OEpartner doesn’t wish to renew his/her OEpartnership after expiration, OEpartner remains the owner of his/her existing allotted units and is fully entitled to the property equivalent to the amount in his property wallet.
  18. Where the need may arise, the company shall correspond with the OEpartner through the contact details as provided in the Registration form.
